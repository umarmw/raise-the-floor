This Christmas RAPP & Code has partnered with Ecologi to ‘Raise The Floor’.
An initiative that plants the square footage of our office space, in the rainforests of Madagascar.
But this isn’t a one-off, in fact we’re pledging to plant the 4,500 trees it takes to fill our offices, every year,
for the next ten years.

Now we’re challenging other brands and agencies to join us.

RAPP & Code’s office space in the UK is 0.45 hectares. Meaning 4,500 trees will be planted.
Costing just £552. We wanted to give more so committed to donating for ten years.
